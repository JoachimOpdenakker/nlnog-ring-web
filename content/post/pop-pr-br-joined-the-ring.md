PoP-PR - AS 10881 - joined the RING today. PoP-PR is the Point of Presence of RNP in Paraná state. We connect various academic and research institutions to the RNP backbone. We are also connected and operate the Paraná Internet Exchange Point.

Users can connect to poppr01.ring.nlnog.net, which is located in Brazil.
