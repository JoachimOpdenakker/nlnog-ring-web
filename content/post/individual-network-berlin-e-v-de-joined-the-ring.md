Individual Network Berlin e.V. - AS 29670 - joined the RING today.

> Individual Network Berlin e.V. (IN-Berlin) is a volunteer driven non-profit. Its purpose is to facilitate non-commercial data communication and provide related services to its participants. Since its inception in 1990 IN-Berlin has always promoted open infrastructures and is currently supporting a number of related groups and internet communities.

Users can connect to inberlin01.ring.nlnog.net, which is located in Germany.
