The eBay Classifieds Group joined the NLNOG RING today. The <a href="http://www.eBayClassifiedsGroup.com">eBay Classifieds Group</a> - AS41552 - is home to some of the best visited websites in Western Europe, so for access providers their participation provides valuable data on how customers will perceive their internet experience.

Ring users can connect to: "ebayclassifiedsgroup01.ring.nlnog.net"
